## 🏛️ Direct Clickable Primary Source Registries

To maintain absolute neutrality, all telemetry inputs must be traceable directly back to these primary sources:

### 1. Electoral Affidavits & Declared Assets
*   **[MyNeta Info / Association for Democratic Reforms (ADR)](https://myneta.info/):** The primary digital database compiling self-sworn candidate affidavits in India, listing assets, liabilities, education, and pending criminal cases.
*   **[Election Commission of India (ECI) Candidate Portal](https://www.eci.gov.in/):** Official repository of legally binding nomination filings and historical election margins.

### 2. Legislative & Performance Telemetry
*   **[PRS Legislative Research](https://prsindia.org/):** Direct, non-partisan database tracking Lok Sabha & Rajya Sabha MPs’ session attendance, debates initiated, questions raised, and bill draft sponsorships.

### 3. Corporate Registries & Network Links
*   **[Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) Portal](https://www.mca.gov.in/):** The official Indian corporate registry. Provides Director Identification Number (DIN) searches and company structural filings.
*   **[OpenCorporates](https://opencorporates.com/):** The world’s largest open database of corporate entities, useful for tracking multinational subsidiaries and board affiliations.

### 4. Judicial & Penal Dossier
*   **[e-Courts India Services](https://ecourts.gov.in/):** National judicial portal tracking active trial registers, court orders, charge sheets, and case updates across district and high courts.

### 5. Offshore Trusts & Shell Entities
*   **[ICIJ Offshore Leaks Database](https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/):** Maintained by the International Consortium of Investigative Journalists. Indexes beneficial ownerships mapped in the Panama, Paradise, and Pandora Papers.

### 6. Public Grievances & Fund Audits
*   **[CPGRAMS Portal](https://pgportal.gov.in/):** Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System tracking resolution rates of constituent complaints.
*   **[MPLADS Fund Portal](https://mplads.gov.in/):** Tracks implementation and audit files for local area development funds allocated to MPs.

### 1. Benami (Proxy) Shareholding & Corporate Address Clustering
*   **The Loophole:** Politicians often registers shell companies using proxies (domestic staff, accountants, or remote cousins) to obscure ownership of high-value real estate or commercial licenses.
*   **Our Verification Strategy:**
    *   **Address Clustering Analysis:** Search business registries to identify different companies sharing the exact same small residential address, email domain, or mobile number in filing histories.
    *   **Unsecured Loan Mapping:** Audit corporate balance sheets for "unsecured loans" from shell entities. High-value loans given to relative-owned firms with zero active commercial operations are primary indicators of laundered funds.
    *   **Paid-up Capital Anomalies:** Flag companies registered with minimal paid-up capital (e.g., ₹1 Lakh) that magically hold assets or properties worth ₹50 Crore.

### 2. Land Registry & Geographic Zoning Correlation
*   **The Loophole:** Acquiring agricultural land cheaply and utilizing executive power to fast-track its conversion to a highly lucrative "commercial/industrial zone," or aligning state expressways directly through those coordinates.
*   **Our Verification Strategy:**
    *   **Geospatial Purchases Overlay:** Map all property purchases disclosed in family ECI affidavits or proxy holdings onto local GIS maps.
    *   **Time-Series Zoning Correlation:** Automatically audit state infrastructure project dates and land-use change permits. Flag cases where agricultural-to-commercial permits were approved within 18 months of a relative's property acquisition.

### 3. The Sub-Contracting Conglomerate Web
*   **The Loophole:** To avoid direct conflict-of-interest tags, a relative's company does not bid directly for major state tenders. Instead, a large, independent conglomerate wins the ₹500 Crore public work contract, and subsequently sub-contracts the earthmoving, material supply, or "consultancy" services to the politician's nephew's firm at highly inflated rates.
*   **Our Verification Strategy:**
